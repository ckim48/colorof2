Solution for colorblind

EnChroma lenses are engineered using a revolutionary lens technology developed in the early 2000’s by a glass scientist and UC Berkeley educated mathematician.

EnChroma develops optical lens technology that selectively filters out wavelengths of light at the point where this confusion or excessive overlap of color sensitivity occurs. The M and L cones are altered in such a way that there is a greater amount of difference in color discrimination along the so-called “confusion line” for that individual.

The lens technology increases contrast between the red and green color signals, alleviating symptoms of color blindness for a richer experience of the world. EnChroma continues to advance the field of color vision research and bring new products to market that solve unique color deficiency issues.
