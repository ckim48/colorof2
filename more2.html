Plant - Pop Art Style

Taeri Kim

The following painting incorporates both the realistic and pop-art aspects from the main object and the background. Drawn with colored pencil and poster color, the artwork presents most vivid and bright hues. Because the background is made up of numerous circles with various colors, one can easily compare how different colors are perceived under the lense of colorblindness.
